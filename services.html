Our services

Cloud Services

The cloud computing model enables convenient, on-demand access to a shared pool of configurable computing resources, which are rapidly provisioned and released with minimal effort.

Cloud services are delivered as either IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS:


  • Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS): Delivers Cloud Infrastructure like servers, storage, network, and operating systems.
  • Software as a Service (SaaS): Software that is built, customized, and delivered to customers on demand, with built-in scalability and flexible billing options.


Kulweb is a provider of Cloud Services, focusing on Microsoft Azure. Azure’s rich collection of toolsets ensures that any cloud solution can be assembled with relative ease, providing a stable and effective solution to most client needs.

Microsoft Azure

Microsoft launched its cloud platform, Azure, in 2010. Azure is currently the only major cloud platform consistently ranked as a leader for IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS. Microsoft continues to use this same platform for its own offerings, such as Xbox Live, Bing, Office 365, SQL, etc.


Microsoft is extensively marketing its cloud offering to enterprises to roll out their own services and applications, with great success.

Unified Communications

Unified communications is a business and marketing concept describing integrating enterprise communication services such as voice, video, personal, team messaging, voicemail, and content sharing. This can include control, management, and integration of these channels. Unified communications allows an individual to send a message on one medium and receive the same communication on another medium. Kulweb Technologies offers unified communications solutions that enable your business to operate quickly and cheaply.
